Auto-Create Tasks for High-Value Opportunities

Business Problem

Sales representatives may forget to create follow-up tasks for important opportunities, resulting in inconsistent sales processes and missed executive oversight for high-value deals.

Solution

Built a Salesforce Record-Triggered Flow that automatically creates different follow-up tasks based on Opportunity Amount.

Opportunities between $10,000 and $49,999 generate a Standard High-Value Review Task.
Opportunities $50,000 and above generate an Executive Review Required Task.
Business Value
Reduces manual work
Standardizes follow-up
Improves executive visibility
Eliminates duplicate automation
Improves maintainability
