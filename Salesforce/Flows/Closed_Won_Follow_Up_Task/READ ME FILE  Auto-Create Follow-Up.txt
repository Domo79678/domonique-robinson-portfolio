# Auto-Create Follow-Up Task on Closed Won Opportunity

## Business Problem
Sales representatives may forget to create follow-up tasks after an opportunity is marked Closed Won.

## Solution
Built an After-Save Record-Triggered Flow that automatically creates a follow-up Task whenever an Opportunity is updated to Closed Won.

## Business Value
- Reduces manual work
- Standardizes follow-up activities
- Improves customer experience
- Increases consistency across the sales process

## Technologies
- Salesforce Flow Builder
- Record-Triggered Flow
- After-Save Automation

## Status
Completed and tested successfully.
