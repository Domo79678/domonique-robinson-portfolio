# JobRadar Case Study

## Project Summary

JobRadar is a Python-based job search assistant that automatically analyzes Gmail job alerts, scores opportunities, identifies recruiter outreach, and generates actionable reports.

The goal was to reduce manual job searching and create a repeatable process for prioritizing opportunities aligned with my career goals.

## Business Problem

Job seekers often receive hundreds of alerts from multiple sources, making it difficult to determine which opportunities deserve attention.

Important recruiter messages and high-value roles can easily be missed.

## Solution

I built JobRadar to:

* Connect directly to Gmail
* Extract job opportunities from alerts
* Score jobs against career targets
* Detect recruiter outreach
* Categorize opportunities by priority
* Export Excel reports for action

## Results

Current version includes:

* Gmail API integration
* LinkedIn Job Alert ingestion
* Google Job Alert ingestion
* Job scoring engine
* Resume recommendation engine
* Recruiter lead detection
* Excel dashboard reporting
