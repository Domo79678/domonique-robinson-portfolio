# JobRadar Case Study

## Project Summary

JobRadar is a Python-based job search assistant that connects to Gmail and analyzes job alert emails from LinkedIn and Google Job Alerts.

The tool extracts job opportunities, scores them against target career paths, recommends resume versions, detects recruiter outreach, and generates action-focused reports.

## Business Problem

Job seekers often receive a high volume of job alerts from multiple platforms. Reviewing each email manually is time consuming and makes it easy to miss strong opportunities or recruiter messages.

The problem was not finding jobs. The problem was filtering, prioritizing, and organizing them efficiently.

## Solution

I built JobRadar to automate the job alert review process.

The application connects to Gmail, reads job alert emails, extracts job information, scores opportunities, categorizes them by fit, and exports reports that support faster decision-making.

## Features Built

* Gmail API integration
* OAuth authentication
* LinkedIn Job Alert processing
* Google Job Alert processing
* Job title, company, location, source, and date extraction
* Scoring engine based on target roles
* Career track classification
* Resume recommendation logic
* Application status tracking
* Location filtering
* Company scoring
* Recruiter lead detection
* Excel dashboard reporting
* Weekend apply list generation

## Career Tracks Supported

* Salesforce / CRM
* Sales Ops / RevOps
* Business Analyst
* Data / Reporting

## Business Value

JobRadar helps reduce manual job search effort by turning a cluttered inbox into a prioritized list of opportunities.

Instead of reviewing every job alert manually, the user can focus on Apply Now, High Priority, Weekend Apply List, and Recruiter Leads reports.
