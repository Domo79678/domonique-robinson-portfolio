# Executive Sales Dashboard

## Business Problem

Leadership needs a clear view of regional sales performance, product profitability, and sales representative revenue to make better business decisions.

## Business Questions

* Which region generates the most revenue?
* Which product generates the most profit?
* Which sales representative generates the most revenue?

## Visualizations Created

* Revenue by Region
* Profit by Product
* Revenue by Sales Rep

## Key Insights

* The North region generated the highest revenue at $13,200.
* Laptops generated the highest profit at $3,000.
* Sales Rep R001 generated the highest revenue at $7,200.

## Business Recommendations

* Review the North region’s sales strategy to identify what is driving stronger performance.
* Prioritize laptop sales because they are the strongest profit driver.
* Study the top-performing sales rep’s approach and look for ways to replicate it across the team.
